About Us

The NIT Meghalaya Robotics Club is a student-run organization that aims to promote interest in robotics and related fields among the student community. We provide a platform for students to learn, experiment, and innovate in the field of robotics.

Our club consists of passionate students from various disciplines who work together to design and build robots for various competitions and projects. We participate in national and international robotics competitions, organize workshops and events, and collaborate with industry professionals.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced robotics enthusiast, our club offers opportunities for everyone to learn and grow. Join us in our mission to push the boundaries of technology and innovation!
